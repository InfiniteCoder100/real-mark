About Us

Our Journey

Founded on January 18, 1988, in the heart of Chawni, Real Mark Sports began its journey under the leadership of Late Shri Arjun Das Bhatia. From the very start, we offered a comprehensive range of sports goods, apparel, footwear, and fitness equipment, featuring top brands like SGSS, Yonex, Wilson, TED, Techno, Sega, Nivea, and Vector.

As our reputation grew, we expanded into the educational sector, supplying sports equipment to leading schools such as Emerald Heights, Daly College, ShishuKunj, Satya Sai, Vidyasagar, NDPS, and Queens College, along with several universities and schools.

From year 2000 under the visionary leadership of Mr Vinod Bhatia, Real Mark Sports has seen a growth in many fold. By 2023, we ventured into the wholesale business across Madhya Pradesh, actively working on platforms like GEM Portal.

Apart from sports goods, we specialize in school uniforms and sports uniforms, partnering with prestigious institutions across the region.

Vision & Goals

At Real Mark Sports, our vision goes beyond growth and expansion. We are committed to:

  • Delivering high-quality sports products and services
  • Ensuring 100% customer satisfaction
  • Creating a trusted and customer-centric brand
  • Offering exceptional service and seamless shopping experience
  • Becoming a household name in the sports industry
